Plot summary

Alchemised explores the story of two poeple bound by loss, obsession, and the search for meaning, set in a world where alchemy is botha science and a metephor for human transformation. The novel delves into the alchemy of the soul- how poeple are broken down and rebuilt through experience, love, and pain.

The story begins in a setting where alchemy is both revered and feared, and where every act of creation requires sacrific. Both main characters are driven by greif and longing, trying to relcaim something they've lost- or to turn their suffering into something beautiful. As they cross paths, their relationship becomes a catalyst for change: each forces the other to confront the past, their limits, and what it truly means to be "transformed". Through quiet moments, moral dilemmas, and emotional intensity Alchemised explores how love, knowledge, and ambition can either purify or destroy.

SenLinYu's prose is lyrical and introspective, focusing more on phsycological and emotional alchemy than on action. The result is a story that feels intiment, haunting, and ultimately redemptive.

About SenLinYu

They/them and she/her

SenLinYu grew up in the Pacific Northwest and studied classical liberal arts and culture. they started writing in the Notes app of their phone during their baby's nap time. Their collected online works have garnered more than twenty million individual downloards and have been translated into twenty-three langauges. They live in Portland, Oregon, with their family. Alchemised is their first novel.

SenLinYu is the pseudonymous author best known for the Harry Potter fanfiction Manacled and Alchemised, both of which have gained widespread acclaim within the fanfiction community for their sophisticated prose, emotional depth, and complex worldbuilding.
