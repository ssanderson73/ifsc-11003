About the Book

Alchemised is a romance and fantasy novel by SenLinYu, the auther best known for the fanfiction Manacled. Like much of her work, Alchemised blands intense emotional themese, complex character dynamics, and a touch of the magical or mystical- rooted in deep physchilogical and philosophical understones.
